About Me

Amy Portillo is a dedicated computer science student with a strong focus on web development. She’s passionate about both the technical and creative aspects of building websites, constantly learning new languages and frameworks to enhance her skills. Although she’s not enrolled in Angela Yu’s web development course, she regularly seeks out other resources to deepen her understanding of front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Amy is excited about creating websites that are not only functional but also user-friendly and visually appealing.

Beyond her coursework, Amy is particularly interested in areas like user experience (UX) design and responsive web development, believing that accessibility and usability are key components of great websites. She enjoys taking on personal projects and contributing to open-source communities, constantly refining her abilities and pushing herself to try new challenges. With a growing portfolio of web development work, Amy is determined to turn her passion into a successful career in tech.

Outside of her studies, Amy is an avid cat lover. She enjoys spending time with her cats, who provide a calming and joyful presence in her life. Her love for animals offers her a much-needed break from the intensity of her technical work, and she believes in maintaining a balanced life to stay motivated and creative in her pursuits.
